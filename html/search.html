Petition Validation Helper

To use this tool, open the Data Entry spreadsheet and for a row of data, select across the columns in the order (First Name, Last Name, House Number, Direction, Street Name, Street Type). Copy (Ctrl + C) the data, and paste (Ctrl + V) the data into the Search Term below. Click Submit.

A query against the voter rolls will be conducted. The data you entered will be in the "Actual Data" table. The data from the voter rolls will be underneath that. The quality of the results will be in a header above the table ("exact", "good", or "lastChance") with the quantity of records returned. A green cell indicates an exact match for a field. A yellow cell indicates strong similarity in a given field.
